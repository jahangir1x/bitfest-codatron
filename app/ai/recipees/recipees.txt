1. Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

    2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    1 tsp baking soda
    1 tsp salt
    1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
    3/4 cup granulated sugar
    3/4 cup brown sugar
    1 tsp vanilla extract
    2 large eggs
    2 cups chocolate chips

Method:

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
    Beat butter, sugars, and vanilla in a large bowl until creamy. Add eggs one at a time.
    Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
    Drop spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheets and bake for 8–10 minutes until golden.

2. Rasgulla (Indian Sweet)

Ingredients:

    1 liter milk
    2 tbsp lemon juice
    1 cup sugar
    4 cups water
    2 cardamom pods

Method:

    Boil milk and add lemon juice to curdle it. Strain to collect paneer (cheese).
    Knead the paneer until smooth and form small balls.
    Boil water with sugar and cardamom to make syrup.
    Add paneer balls to the syrup and cook for 10–15 minutes.

3. Rice Pudding (Kheer)

Ingredients:

    1 liter milk
    1/2 cup basmati rice
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/4 tsp cardamom powder
    2 tbsp almonds and pistachios

Method:

    Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
    Boil milk and add rice. Simmer until rice is cooked and mixture thickens.
    Add sugar, cardamom, and nuts. Cook for 5 minutes.

4. Brownies

Ingredients:

    1/2 cup butter
    1 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    1/3 cup cocoa powder
    1/2 cup flour
    1/4 tsp baking powder
    1/4 tsp salt

Method:

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and cocoa powder.
    Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
    Pour batter into a greased pan and bake for 20–25 minutes.

5. Mango Lassi

Ingredients:

    1 cup mango pulp
    1/2 cup yogurt
    1/4 cup milk
    2 tbsp sugar
    Ice cubes

Method:

    Blend all ingredients until smooth.
    Pour into glasses and serve chilled.

Spicy Dishes
1. Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

    500g chicken pieces
    2 onions (chopped)
    2 tomatoes (chopped)
    2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    1 tsp turmeric
    1 tsp red chili powder
    1 tsp garam masala
    2 tbsp oil

Method:

    Heat oil and sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste.
    Add tomatoes, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook until oil separates.
    Add chicken, cover, and cook until tender. Finish with garam masala.

2. Spicy Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

    200g paneer (cubed)
    1/2 cup yogurt
    1 tsp red chili powder
    1/2 tsp turmeric
    1 tsp garam masala
    1 tbsp lemon juice
    1 tbsp oil

Method:

    Marinate paneer with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for 30 minutes.
    Skewer paneer and grill or bake until golden.

3. Spicy Fried Rice

Ingredients:

    2 cups cooked rice
    1 onion (chopped)
    1/2 cup mixed vegetables
    1 tbsp soy sauce
    1 tsp red chili sauce
    1 tsp garlic (minced)
    2 tbsp oil

Method:

    Heat oil and sauté garlic and onions.
    Add vegetables, sauces, and cooked rice. Stir-fry on high heat.

4. Hot and Sour Soup

Ingredients:

    4 cups vegetable stock
    1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
    1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)
    1 tbsp soy sauce
    1 tsp chili sauce
    1 tsp vinegar
    1 tbsp cornstarch (dissolved in water)

Method:

    Boil stock and add vegetables, soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar.
    Thicken with cornstarch slurry.

5. Spicy Lamb Kebabs

Ingredients:

    500g minced lamb
    1 onion (finely chopped)
    1 tsp red chili powder
    1 tsp cumin powder
    1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    1 tbsp lemon juice
    2 tbsp oil

Method:

    Mix all ingredients and form into kebabs.
    Grill or fry until cooked through.
